Frequently Asked Questions

How does embroidery affect fit?

Embroidery adds minimal weight and structure. For small designs, there's no fit impact. For large designs (4"+ diameter), we recommend sizing up by one size for optimal comfort.

Can I change my size after ordering?

Yes! If your order hasn't started production (within 48 hours), we can change the size free of charge. Contact us immediately after placing your order.

What if the item doesn't fit?

We offer free exchanges within 30 days of delivery. Custom embroidered items (non-returnable) can be exchanged for different sizes at no cost.

How will embroidery look on different sizes?

Our designs are scaled appropriately for each size to maintain visual balance. A 3" design might be 2.5" on XS and 3.5" on XXL. We can customize this upon request.
